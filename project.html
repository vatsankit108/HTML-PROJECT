About Us

We are a community for social service. Anyone who faces any issue related to any government department is welcome file their complaint to us.

Our Objective

Our objective is to serve as an easy and accesible platform between the commom people and the government authorities. We ensure that the the complaint lodged on our website remains confidential. We will make sure that the complaint gets transfered to the concerned authorities as soon as possible. THANK YOU
